## 1. Transaction Cost Engine

Create:

```text id="gq2"
execution/cost_models.py
```

Model:

```text id="gq3"
Brokerage
STT
Exchange Fees
SEBI Charges
GST
Stamp Duty
```

Then:

```text id="gq4"
BUY ₹50,000
Actual Cost ₹50,085

SELL ₹55,000
Actual Proceeds ₹54,900
```

This affects both:

* paper trading
* backtests

Most important missing realism feature.
