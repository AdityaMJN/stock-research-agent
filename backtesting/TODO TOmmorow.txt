## Step 2 — Save Daily Snapshot

Run:

```sql
SELECT
    l.symbol,
    sr.score
FROM screener_results sr
JOIN listings l
    ON l.id = sr.listing_id
WHERE sr.screener_name = 'COMBINED_RANKING'
ORDER BY sr.score DESC
LIMIT 20;
```

Save output into:

```text
reports/
    combined_ranking_YYYY_MM_DD.csv
```

This becomes your first research archive.
